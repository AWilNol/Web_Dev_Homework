Meat Lovers Pizza Stuffed Peppers

Here is what you will need for this yummy looking recipe:

  • 4 oz Bacon, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
  • 1 lb Ground Italian sausage
  • 1 small Onion, diced
  • 2 cloves Garlic, chopped
  • 4 oz Pepperoni, sliced, or mini
  • 1/4 c Black Olives, sliced
  • 2 c Pizza Sauce or Marinara Sauce
  • 4 large Bell Peppers, cut in half, seeds removed
  • 2 c Mozzarella, shredded
  • 1 c Mushrooms, or as you like
