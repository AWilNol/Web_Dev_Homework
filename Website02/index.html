Grilled Cheese Sandwich!

Here's how you make this delicious sandwich!

  1. Heat frying pan large enough for the two slices bread to medium heat. Add some butter to pan.

  2. Spread butter on outer side of two pieces bread, as shown in first image.

  3. Once butter is melted, add bread, buttered side down, as shown in second (lower) image.

  4. Add favored cheese slice, ie. sliced cheddar, american, provlone, swiss, or muenster, as shown in third (upper) image.

  5. You can also add a slice of your favorite deli meat, or pre-cooked bacon atop cheese. Optional.

  6. Let cheese soften, but keep an eye on the toastiness of the other side of bread. It can burn fast.

  7. When to desired toastiness level, flip bread slices to join as shown in last (lower) image.

  8. Remove from and turn off the stove.

And you'll have a delicious grilled cheese sandwich like the image below- Ta-Daaaa!!
